IUOE Local 564

AMENTUM / NASA-JSC · SEP 2025 – AUG 2028

Preamble / Purpose of Agreement

This Agreement is made and entered into by and between Amentum Service, Inc., hereinafter referred to as the "Company" and International Union of Operating Engineers, Local No. 564, AFL-CIO, hereinafter referred to as the "Union."

This Agreement is entered into under the terms of the National Labor Relations Act, as amended, in the mutual interest of present and future employees, to promote the Safety and continuity of operations and maintenance, to stabilize employment under reasonable hours, rates of pay, working conditions and in the interest of the Plant Operation effort of the United States Government, to further efficiency and economy of operations. It is recognized by the Agreement to be the duty of the Company and the Union to cooperate fully both individually and collectively for the advancement of said conditions.

EFFECTIVE: SEPTEMBER 01, 2025 THROUGH AUGUST 31, 2028
